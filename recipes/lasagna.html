Lasagna

Description

This classic lasagna recipe is rich and satisfying, perfect for family dinners.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 3 cups mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large pot, cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a skillet over medium heat, add olive oil and sauté minced garlic until fragrant.
  4. Add ground beef to the skillet, season with salt and pepper, and cook until browned. Drain excess fat.
  5. Stir in marinara sauce with the cooked beef and let it simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of the meat sauce.
  7. Place a layer of lasagna noodles over the sauce.
  8. Spread a layer of ricotta cheese over the noodles.
  9. Sprinkle a layer of mozzarella cheese over the ricotta.
  10. Repeat the layers (sauce, noodles, ricotta, mozzarella) until all ingredients are used, ending with a layer of mozzarella and Parmesan on top.
  11. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  12. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until the cheese is golden and bubbly.
  13. Let the lasagna cool for 15 minutes before serving.

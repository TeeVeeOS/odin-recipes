Pancakes

Description

These classic fluffy pancakes are perfect for a delightful breakfast or brunch.

Ingredients

  • 1½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 3½ teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 1¼ cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons melted butter

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt.
  2. Make a well in the center and pour in the milk, egg, and melted butter; mix until smooth.
  3. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat.
  4. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately ¼ cup for each pancake.
  5. Brown on both sides and serve hot with your favorite toppings.

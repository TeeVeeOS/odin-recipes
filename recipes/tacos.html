Tacos

Description

These classic beef tacos are flavorful and easy to make, perfect for a quick weeknight dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 12 taco shells
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup chopped onions

Steps

  1. In a skillet over medium heat, cook the ground beef until browned. Drain any excess fat.
  2. Add the taco seasoning to the beef, following the package instructions. Simmer for a few minutes.
  3. While the beef is simmering, warm the taco shells according to the package instructions.
  4. Assemble the tacos by filling each shell with the seasoned beef, then topping with lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, onions, and a dollop of sour cream.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy!
